What is IAMS?

Solving the attachment
placement problem

The Department of Computer Science previously handled industrial attachment manually — stacks of paperwork, missed deadlines, and inconsistent placements. IAMS replaces this with a centralized digital system where students, organizations, and coordinators all work from one platform.

Students

Register, list your skills and preferences, browse organizations, and track your placement status in real time.

Organizations

Register, specify required skills, set available positions, and receive matched students automatically.

Coordinators

Manage registrations, run the matching algorithm, confirm placements, and monitor the full process.
